The Beginning

Born a curious child, I loved to tinker with electrical appliances just to see how they worked underneath. I always wanted to know how things worked and why. Eventually I saw my first computer, and it was love at first sight. I was really passionate about computers as I would happily read computer text books for fun and I even got an award for the best computer science student in my class back in secondary school.

The Calling

I love solving peoples problems and giving them value. I'm also an entrepreneur by the way. Being a computer scientist and entrepreneur is amazing as it allows you to solve problems directly. I'm one who is constantly looking for problems to solve with the computer.

The Future

I'm on a journey to become a world class programmer that builds products that solve problems and add value to peoples lives.
